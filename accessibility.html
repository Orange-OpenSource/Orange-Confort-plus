Accessibility statement for Orange Comfort+

Context

Orange is committed to making its websites, mobile applications and digital street furniture accessible in accordance with article 106 of law no. 2016-1321 of October 7, 2016 for a digital Republic, as described in its multi-year plan aiming to improve digital accessibility (PDF, 230KB).

Conformance status

Comfort+ is not compliant with WCAG 2.2, because there's currently no audit results.

Feedback and contact information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this site. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility problems by sending an email to confort.plus@orange.com.
