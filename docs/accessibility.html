Introduction

Orange is committed to making its websites, mobile applications and digital street furniture accessible in accordance with article 106 of law no. 2016-1321 of October 7, 2016 for a digital Republic, as described in its multi-year plan aiming to improve digital accessibility (PDF, 230KB, in french language).

Scope of the assessment

The evaluation has been conducting on the following sample of web pages:

  1. Confort+ : https://confort-plus.orange.com/

Conformance status

The audit reports a mean compliance of 66% with 10 non-compliance.

A compliance score is measured for each audited page: the score equals the sum of compliant criteria divided by the number of criteria applicable to the page.
