Privacy of Your Personal Data on Orange Comfort+

This page aims to inform you about the collection of your personal data by Orange Comfort+ and how it is used by the extension.

The Orange Confort+ extension does not collect any personal data.

The User's data remains on their device. For the extension to function properly, it must store the following data locally on the user's PC:

  • The usage mode selected by the user.
  • The user's configuration preferences: visual interface settings (text colors, text size, pointer color, etc.), audio interface settings (type of vocalization), and control settings (scrollbar, shortcut buttons, etc.).

These data are not collected by Orange Innovation Produits & Services.
